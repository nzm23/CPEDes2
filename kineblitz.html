KINEBLITZ

LOREM IPSUM

“Kineblitz,” a biofeedback game-based habilitation system designed specifically for children that are diagnosed with spastic cerebral palsy for the upper body. The game will be structured in such a way that it employs electromyographic feedback. By initially devising a microcontroller-based device capable of serving as a surface electromyography (EMG) sensor, muscle activity from the surface of the upper area of the body that is above the muscle on the skin shall be recorded and will serve as an input to be utilized to control the game.ss
